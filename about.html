Live your legacy

The founder of the Monique Lhuillier brand is the Filipino-born designer Monique Lhuillier. Her story largely repeats the story of Vera Wong, who could not find a wedding dress for herself and decided to create her own wedding dress.

Likewise, having developed dress options for herself and her invited girlfriends, she decided that she could create dresses for women all over the world, and she was not mistaken.

Not much time has passed, and now her boutiques are open in Tokyo, New York, Paris and London, and the designer herself is known throughout the world as the creator of the most sophisticated wedding dresses.

At the same time, she regularly releases ready-to-wear lines and evening collections, and her dresses can be seen not only at weddings, but also on the red carpet. The designer prefers the most expensive materials and does not skimp on French lace, delicate silk and brocade, actively uses hand embroidery and expensive inlays.

History

1987

2021

‘1987

The "Love Story" trade mark appeared on the USA market in 1987 as a small manufacturer of wedding clothes. The idea of ​​creating a fashion house was very simple - to give people elegant beauty, while not overloading the dresses with details. Our task was to create a unique product, which, having its own style, corresponds to the world trends in wedding fashion

The idea to go into the wedding business came after my friend started preparing for the wedding. And from the very beginning of the preparation, she faced a problem in choosing a dress, finding neither a suitable salon nor service.

At that time, we had a team of like-minded people headed by my husband Viktor and we thought: why not open that very ideal wedding salon? We started to do this, and everything gradually began to take shape. The main difficulties are not the simple financial situation in the world in 1987 and the instability of the exchange rate, since our entire business is tied to partnership with Europe and America.
